Spanish or English?



how to play "Spanish or English?"

  1. A random word will appear in the box.
  2. Guess if the word is Spanish, English, or Both.
  3. Click the Spanish, English, or Both button.
  4. See instant feedback:
    • "Correct!" → You guessed right!
    • "Nope! It's ..." → You guessed wrong.
  5. Your score updates automatically.
  6. Click Next Word to try another word.
  7. Keep playing to see how many words you can guess correctly!
